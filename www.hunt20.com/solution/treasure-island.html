Written by Ty

First solve the jigsaw puzzle. Reading the letters crossed by the blue line gives "COMPLETE THIS STAR BATTLE".
We can now treat the board as a star battle with the blocks given. The letters at the stars are "THE ANSWER IS WHIRLPOOL", giving us WHIRLPOOL.
